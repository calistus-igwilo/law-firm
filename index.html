Our Practices Areas

Corporate Law

Criminal Law

Intellectual Property Law

Our intellectual property practice encompasses trademark and patent registration, advisory services, and litigation for diverse clients, notably winning a significant moral rights case for an author and pursuing a major passing off claim against a communication giant in Nigeria.

Constitutional Law

Our firm has extensive experience in constitutional law, from defending states in revenue allocation disputes and challenging constitutional interpretations, to protecting clients' fundamental rights through enforcement procedures and litigation under the Freedom of Information Act
